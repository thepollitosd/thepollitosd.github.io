Senator Evelyn Reed

Party: Independent

Current Office: U.S. Senator for the State of New Columbia

First Elected to Current Office: November 2018

Biography

Senator Evelyn Reed was born on June 15, 1975, in Capital City, New Columbia. After graduating with honors from State University with a degree in Political Science and later earning a Juris Doctor from National Law School, Reed began her career as a public defender. Driven by a passion for community advocacy, she successfully ran for City Council in 2008, where she championed initiatives for urban renewal and education reform.

Her pragmatic approach and dedication to constituent services led to a successful bid for the State Legislature in 2012. As a State Representative, she focused on fiscal responsibility and environmental protection. In 2018, Senator Reed was elected to the U.S. Senate, where she continues to advocate for bipartisan solutions to national challenges, with a particular focus on healthcare access and technological innovation.
